What Is Ramshackle?

Ramshackle is an app created to help you keep track of your home maintenance repairs and manage documents and notes associated with those projects.
We understand the challenges of maintaining and repairing your home. Our app keeps you organized so you can focus on keeping your home standing.

Ramshackle Features

  • Create project folders
  • Upload invoices
  • Keep detailed notes

How To Use Ramshackle

Enter your email and password to login to your Ramshackle account. Click Sign up instead to create a new account. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to you upon completing the registration fields and submitting them.

Upon logging in, your main dashboard will display room options where you can store your projects.

Selecting a room will open a summary of existing projects and allow you to create new ones.

Click View next to a project to view the details and uploaded images or invoices. Details can be modified for projects and new images can be uploaded.

The Ramshackle logo in the upper left corner of the screen will open a navigation menu where you can update your user details, search for projects, modify your personal settings, or logout of your account.

The settings in your Ramshackle account can be personalized to send you push notifications or toggle light/dark mode.
